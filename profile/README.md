## 🧠 About Us

TatvaMind Intelligence Lab is an independent AI research lab focused on building intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and operate in real-world environments.

We work at the intersection of **machine learning, deep learning, generative AI, and autonomous agents**, with a strong emphasis on practical implementation and scalable system design.

Our mission is to advance artificial intelligence through **research, experimentation, and real-world applications** that enhance human capabilities.
